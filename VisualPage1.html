Cloudiness

City Latitude versus Cloudiness - There are various trends to be noted here. It seems that it becomes more cloudy as the latitude increases. It is possible that it is less cloudy as the latitude decreases. It is more heavily cloudy as the latitude increasses for reasons having to do with location and weather conditions. As cloudiness decreases to zero, there is more zero cloudiness on the positive side of equator - or the Northern Hemisphere.
