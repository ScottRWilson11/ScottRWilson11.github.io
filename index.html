City Latitude versus X

Summary - The summary of the project includes a study of weather patterns across 500 cities. This was accomplished through the OpenWeatherMap API. The study was conducted over the areas of cloudiness, humidy, max temperature, and wind speeds. Graphs were plotted indicating the trends across these factors over Latitude thus highlighting Southern versus Northern Hemispheres over the Earth's surface.
